!!Bingo Game Rules!!

Objective:

The objective of the game is to be the first player to mark five numbers in a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) on your board.

Gameplay:

For Easy and Medium Level:

1. You have a 5x5 Bingo board with numbers ranging from 1 to 25. Each number is randomly placed on the board.
2. Players take turns selecting numbers from their board and "cutting" them(Either you start the Match or Bot).
3. Click on a number to mark it. The goal is to form a straight line with five marked numbers.
4. Remember when another player marks a number, then you have to mark it on your board, Otherwise it will be your loss.
5. You can achieve a straight line horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
6. The first player to complete a valid straight line of five marked numbers will be the winner!.

For Hard Level:

1. You have a 5x5 Bingo board with numbers ranging from 1 to 25. Each number is randomly placed on the board.
2. Players take turns selecting numbers from their board and "cutting" them(Either you start the Match or Bot).
3. Click on a number to mark it. The goal is to form a five straight line with marked numbers.
4. Remember when another player marks a number, then you have to mark it on your board, Otherwise it will be your loss.
5. You can achieve a straight line horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
6. The first player to complete a valid five straight line of marked numbers will be the winner!.
